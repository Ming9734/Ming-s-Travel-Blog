About Me

A traveler who loves exploring Europe slowly, one city at a time.

Hello, I’m Ming!

Welcome to my travel blog. This website is a collection of memories from my slow travels across Europe. I enjoy walking through old streets, visiting museums, and discovering stories hidden in cities and towns.

Every trip teaches me something new — about history, culture, and myself. I hope my notes, photos, and maps can inspire you to start your own journey.
